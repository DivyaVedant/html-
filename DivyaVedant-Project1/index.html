About Us

Our Cafe is a community-focused cafe that strives to create nutritious, simple and delicious food paired with exceptional coffee. Taking inspiration from India's forward-thinking culinary scene, Our Cafe aims to be a place for the community near and far to come unwind in the light-filled space.

RESERVATIONS & AVAILABILITY

Our Cafe is first come, first serve but we do take limited reservations exclusively for tables of 6 or more for our evening service. Please visit us at the restaurant to join the waitlist, as we are unable to take names over email or phone. For weekend brunch we seat exclusively on a walk-in basis. For additional questions, please email us at the specific location you booked at (email list below)

RECEIPT REQUESTS

For Receipt Requests, please email us at the specific location you visited (email list below) with date, name, amount and last four digits of the credit card provided.

CREDIT CARD CHARGE INQUIRIES

For Credit Card Charge inquiries, please allow two business days for your bank to settle all pending charges. If charges are still not correct, please email us at the specific location you visited (email list below) so we can correct any errors.

LOST AND FOUND

For Lost and Found requests please email us at the specific location you visited (email list below) with the following information: date and time of dining, area in which you were seated, description of item, and reservation name.
